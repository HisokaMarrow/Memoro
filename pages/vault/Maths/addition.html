Memory Association Technique

To significantly boost your memory, use the Association Technique. Instead of trying to remember each word separately, turn them into vivid mental images and link them together into a creative story.

For example, if the words are "cloud", "piano", "tree":
Imagine a fluffy cloud playing a piano in the sky, and then that piano jumps down and starts climbing a tree.

Try to visualize the story. Feel the texture, hear the sounds, and involve your senses. The more bizarre or emotional the story, the more likely it will stick in your memory.

Now that you’ve learned the technique — give it another shot!
