Doomsday Calculation

The Doomsday Algorithm is a mental method for determining the day of the week for any given date — past, present, or future.

It works by anchoring your calculation to "Doomsday" dates — fixed calendar days that always fall on the same weekday within each year. With a bit of training, you can calculate weekdays in seconds using nothing but your mind.

This system is used by mental athletes, memory champions, and anyone who loves a good brain trick. It's fast, fun, and incredibly useful.
